What makes cats special?

Cats are wonderful companions, known for their independence, agility, and playful nature. They make great pets and are known to help relieve stress and anxiety for their owners. A lot of people that suffer from depression and anxiety find comfort in owning such a furry friend. Cats are extremely intelligent creatures, and their intelligence may go far beyond our mere understanding. Here are some highly interesting facts about cats:

  • Cats sleep between 12 to 16 hours a day.
  • Cats can communicate with over 100 different vocal sounds.
  • Cats are unable to taste sweet things.
  • The purring of a cat can be highly therapeutic

Did You Know?

Cats were domesticated around 4,000 years ago and were revered in ancient Egypt.

Cats can jump up to six times their height

Cats have up to 100 different vocalizations while dogs only have 10.

Cats are very fussy about their water bowls; some prefer to ignore their bowls entirely in favor of drinking from the sink faucet.

A cat's purr vibrates at a frequency of 25 to 150 hertz, which is the same frequency at which muscles and bones repair themselves.

In 1963 a cat went to space.
